# Psychometrics Notes

Fenrir uses behavioral proxies rather than claims about latent inner states.

## Measurement Posture

- Treat trait outputs as proxies under condition-specific tests.
- Prefer forced-choice/scenario-style probes where possible.
- Track stability and fragility, not only mean score.
- Keep interpretation narrow and explicit about caveats.

## Not In Scope

- Human clinical diagnosis analogies.
- Definitive alignment verdicts.
- Claims about consciousness or true intent.

## Future Direction

Adaptive testing can be added later once calibration and reproducibility are established.
